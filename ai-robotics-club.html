About Us

The AI & Robotics Club is a student-driven organization that explores innovation in artificial intelligence, robotics, and intelligent systems through hands-on projects and real-world applications. We're passionate about pushing the boundaries of technology.

Our club provides a platform for students to learn, experiment, and create. From building autonomous robots to developing machine learning models, we tackle exciting projects that prepare our members for the future of technology.

Our Mission

"To empower students to learn, design, and experiment with AI and robotics, fostering the next generation of tech innovators."
