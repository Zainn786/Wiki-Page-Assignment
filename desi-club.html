About Us

The Desi Club is a vibrant cultural club that celebrates the rich traditions, delicious food, music, and festivals of South Asian countries. We create a welcoming space where students can connect with their roots while sharing their culture with others.

Whether you're from the subcontinent or simply curious about South Asian culture, our doors are always open. We believe in building friendships through shared experiences, laughter, and of course, amazing food!

Our Mission

"To foster cultural awareness and community spirit in a welcoming space where both DESI and non-DESI students can celebrate, participate, and feel at home."
