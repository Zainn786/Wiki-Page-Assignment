About Us

The Environmental and Animal Welfare Club (EAWC) is a passionate community of students dedicated to promoting sustainability and protecting our planet. We believe that small actions can lead to significant change, and we're committed to making RIT Dubai a greener campus.

Our club encourages students to protect natural resources, support animal wellbeing, and take meaningful action for environmental conservation. We create a space where like-minded individuals can come together to learn, discuss, and act on pressing environmental issues.

Our Mission

"To empower students to become responsible stewards of the planet by raising awareness and fostering compassion for all living creatures."
