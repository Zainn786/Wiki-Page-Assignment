Women in Engineering (WIE)

RIT Dubai — IEEE Affinity Group

A supportive IEEE affinity group dedicated to empowering women in STEM by creating an inclusive space for learning, collaboration, and growth. WIE at RIT Dubai focuses on inspiring and uplifting the next generation of women leaders, driving positive change in engineering and beyond.

Overview

Women in Engineering (WIE) at RIT Dubai is a student-led IEEE affinity group that supports, motivates, and connects women pursuing engineering and technology fields. The club provides opportunities for professional development, community building, and technical growth.

Mission

To empower women in STEM through education, mentorship, inspiration, and leadership development. The group strives to build confidence, strengthen technical skills, and encourage collaboration across the engineering community.

Workshops & Skill‑Building Sessions

WIE organizes hands-on sessions focused on technical growth, professional development, and soft‑skill enhancement. These include:

  • Engineering software training (CAD, coding, simulations)
  • Career development workshops (CV building, interview prep)
  • Leadership and communication skill sessions
  • STEM outreach and collaborative learning activities

Networking & Mentorship

The club regularly hosts mentorship circles, alumni meetups, and networking gatherings to connect students with women leaders in STEM across the UAE.

Major Events

  • Spotlight Sessions: Talks featuring women engineers and industry professionals.
  • WIE Networking Gatherings: Community‑building meetups and alumni connections.
  • Collaborative Engineering Projects: Team‑based initiatives encouraging skill growth and problem‑solving.

Impact

Through consistent engagement, WIE has strengthened the sense of belonging among women in STEM at RIT Dubai. Members gain confidence, mentorship, and opportunities that help shape their academic and professional journeys.
