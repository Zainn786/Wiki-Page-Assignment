About Us

Women in Engineering (WIE) is a supportive IEEE affinity group dedicated to empowering women in STEM by creating an inclusive space for learning, collaboration, and growth. We believe that diversity drives innovation and excellence in engineering.

Our club provides mentorship, professional development, and networking opportunities that help women thrive in technical fields. We welcome allies and supporters who share our vision of gender equality in STEM.

Our Mission

"To inspire and uplift the next generation of women leaders, driving positive change in engineering and beyond."
